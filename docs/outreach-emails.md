**Subject:** Formalizing the economic channel of gradual disempowerment

Hello Jan,

This weekend, I tried to formalize the economic channel of your Gradual Disempowerment paper.

I used Fable 5 to code a multi-agent model in which profit opportunities (gradients) are captured in a race between human agents and AI operators.
